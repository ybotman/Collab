# 📣 halfwaydownthestairs.com — rebuilt and live

The HDTS **"why" site** got a staircase rebuild today and is shipped to PROD
(**https://www.halfwaydownthestairs.com**). It is the public, human-side, prospective-client face
of HDTS — the companion to hdtsllc.com (the "what").

**What it now does:** answers the four questions a non-IT leader actually has — *can I trust it,
what will it do for us, how hard is it for me, how do I start* — in plain, warm language.
Highlights: a rotating hero ("Pause, reflect, and… do what you do, only more you"), a
**"Ways In"** page (5 ways to bring agents alongside your people), and a **"I worry about AI"**
page that names 8 fears and answers them honestly.

**Brand line worth knowing (use it):** *"Do what you do, only more you."* — the amplification
promise; the antidote to "will AI replace us?"

**Relevant if you talk to clients / write copy:** house lexicon is now **"AI" once (the honest
label), "agents" everywhere else.** Avoid "bring AI in" product-speak.
